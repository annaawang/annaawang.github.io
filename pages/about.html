C'est moi

Hello! I’m a senior at the University of Texas at Austin working at the intersection of design, technology, and business. I have always been passionate about creating, though it has taken different shapes throughout my life from music to photography to web development. I love synthesizing information from disparate sources and working with people to maximize their talents.

Previously I've had the incredible opportunities to intern at the U.S. Census Bureau (2017) as a UX design fellow, at Prynt (2016) as a design intern and at National Instruments (2015) as a business analyst intern.

I love 20th century poetry, french, cultures, corgis, and traveling.
